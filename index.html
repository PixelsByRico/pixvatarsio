About Pixvatars



Pixvatars is a pfp NTF project inspired by my love for pixelart. I have created 1000 unique characters in 3 months. Each one hand drawn. My end goal is creating the game(s) and digital world I always wanted to. The avatars are useful for the Pixvatars Island Metaverse which is launching Q1 2022. I got into pixelart again due to a challenge hosted by Pixelmap. That's how I got inspired to do it on chain and see how far I can go with only 16x16 pixels. Pixelmap owners got early Whitelist access.




There's a few "easter eggs" + rarities added:


1. Jewelery + Accessories. On the Islands there is going to be 2 main resources: Coconuts and Golden nuggets. Having an avatar with jewelry will give you benefits for the gold resources on the Island


2. Families. Within the 1000 pieces there's a few forming families. Owning a family will add some priveleges with the Island opening in 2022. And might get you a personal gift by the creator *wink*


3. Backgrounds. Each avatar has a background. There's Common, Rare and Unique backgrounds.
